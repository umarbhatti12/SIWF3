About Us

The Saima Ifrahim Welfare Foundation (SIWF) is a non-profit organization founded in August 2023. We are driven by a mission to transform lives through education, women empowerment, livelihood opportunities, and healthcare assistance. Our goal is to nurture skills, confidence, and compassion within every individual we serve.
